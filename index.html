The Power of Touch

Leprosy: An "Eradicated" Disease?

Touch is a very important sense for any being on Earth. A mother’s touch comforts a newborn with her magical love the moment you are born. A father’s hand guides the toddler’s first steps in the world. As a young adult, you are swarmed by hugs, high-fives, and handshakes as you make a name for yourself. Now imagine, for once, all these interactions cease. No one speaks to you, people shun you in front of your face, and you are never again able to feel the touch of those you love. Clearly, ‘touch’ is a very inevitable aspect of our lives, but, devastatingly, thousands of leprosy victims are deprived of this basic necessity. Read through to learn more about this disease and how YOU can make a difference in someone's life today!
